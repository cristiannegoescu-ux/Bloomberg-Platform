Why a tiered model?

A tiered subscription model works inside Ubisoft the same way it works externally: teams adopt at the level they're ready for and grow into the platform. It also creates a clear roadmap for conversations with directors — each tier represents a concrete milestone, not just a vague "future vision." And if we ever license outside Ubisoft, the model is already there.
